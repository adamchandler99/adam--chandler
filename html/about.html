About Me

I was raised in Draper, Utah in a family of eight. I have four brothers and one sister. Something that shaped me growing up is that I am the runt of my litter. Though I am 6'2", I am 2 inches shorter than my next shortest brother.

Because I am smaller, I always felt that I had to do more than my brothers to achieve the same outcome. I hated this growing up, but it's made me very scrappy.

I am a family man. My wife, Mallory, and I were married in February of 2021 at the age of 21. We are expecting our first child in June of 2024. We love playing board/table games and getting outdoors. Here's a photo of us preparing for our first sky-dive.
